About me
Creative, analytical, innovative
Hi! I'm Gabriele Felici, a Food Technologist with a passion for programming and cooking. I live in Lonate Pozzolo, with my dog, Artù. I love being surrounded by nature, hiking through mountain trails, and finding inspiration in the little things around me. When I'm not working, I enjoy dedicating my time to programming and drawing, two of my greatest passions. I truly believe that creativity, in all its forms, is a powerful tool to tackle everyday challenges, whether it's in the kitchen, developing software, or on a canvas.
Career
Food Technologist
After earning a diploma in Hospitality and a Bachelor's degree in Food Science and Technology (University of Milan), I decided to broaden my education with a Master’s in "Food and Society" at the University of Milan-Bicocca. This path has given me a wide and multidisciplinary perspective on the dynamics of the food sector, allowing me to better understand the challenges and opportunities in this ever-evolving field. I worked as a Food Technologist Consultant at R&D Food Tech, BEKO Europe, where I collaborated with international teams on innovative projects focused on developing and optimizing food technologies. My main goal has always been product quality while simultaneously improving processes and ensuring efficiency. I firmly believe that collaboration and the exchange of diverse perspectives are essential to creating solutions that meet the needs of a constantly evolving global market. My passion for the food industry and my commitment to innovation drive me to always seek new ways to improve.
