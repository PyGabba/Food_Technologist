L.I.P. - Learning In Progress
The L.I.P. project operates at the intersection of food and education, with a strong commitment to fostering social and labor inclusion for individuals with special needs or in vulnerable conditions. It serves as a dynamic meeting point where businesses, the public, and users converge to access products and services, as well as participate in specialized training programs. These programs are designed to facilitate collaborations that meet the specific needs of both employers and employees, ensuring a more inclusive and effective integration into the workforce. What sets the enterprise apart is its permeability to institutions, media, and supporters, actively engaging these actors in shared activities and networking. This openness is a key resource, enabling the enterprise to create an accessible space that promotes inclusivity and collaboration. By establishing a robust network of partnerships, the company can provide a service that is truly within reach for all, continually evolving to meet the needs of its different customers. From both structural and product perspectives, the enterprise is dedicated to usability and accessibility. These principles drive the development of services aimed at creating opportunities for individuals in marginalized positions, while continuously improving to maintain high standards of service delivery.
